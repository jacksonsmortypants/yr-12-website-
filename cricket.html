Cricket is a bat-and-ball game for two teams of 11 players, where the batting team scores runs by hitting a bowled ball and running between wickets, while the fielding team tries to dismiss the batters and prevent runs. The game has a governing body, the International Cricket Council (ICC), and its rules are known as the "Laws of Cricket", maintained by the Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC).
